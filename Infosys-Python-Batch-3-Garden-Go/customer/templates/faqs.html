Garden Go Auto Courier - Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs for Customers

1. How do I place an order for courier services?

You can place an order by visiting our website and filling out the courier request form with the necessary details such as pickup and delivery address, package size, and any specific instructions.

2. What types of items can I send through your courier service?

We handle a wide range of items, from small packages to larger garden-related products. Please ensure your item is well-packed and does not violate any shipping regulations.

3. How do I track my package?

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking ID to track your package through our website or app in real-time.

4. Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, you can change the delivery address by contacting our customer service before the package is dispatched. After dispatch, changes may not be possible.

5. What are the payment options available?

We accept payments via credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery.

6. How do I know if my order has been successfully placed?

After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation email and SMS with your order details and tracking number.

7. What should I do if my package is delayed?

Please contact our customer support team immediately if your package is delayed, and we will investigate the issue and keep you updated.

8. Is there an option to schedule a delivery time?

Yes, we offer scheduled delivery times based on availability. Please select your preferred time when placing the order.

9. Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it is dispatched. Once dispatched, cancellation may not be possible, but please contact support for assistance.

10. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or live chat on our website.

11. Can I send perishable items?

We do not recommend sending perishable items as our service does not guarantee timely delivery under certain conditions.

12. Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer domestic courier services. However, we are working on expanding to international deliveries.

13. What should I do if the recipient is unavailable during delivery?

If the recipient is unavailable, we will attempt to reschedule the delivery. If we cannot reach them, the package will be returned.

14. Are there any restrictions on package size or weight?

Yes, we have size and weight limitations. Please check our website for detailed guidelines on package dimensions and weight.

15. How do I request a refund?

If your package was lost or damaged, you can request a refund by contacting our customer support with your order details.

FAQs for Couriers

1. How do I become a courier for Garden Go Auto Courier?

To become a courier, visit our website and fill out the registration form with your details. We will review your application and provide training to help you get started.

2. What is the pay structure for couriers?

Couriers are paid based on distance, package weight, and the urgency of the delivery. You will receive payment weekly for completed deliveries.

3. Do I need a specific vehicle to deliver packages?

While having a vehicle is beneficial, we accept couriers with different types of transportation, including bicycles, motorcycles, and cars, depending on the size and weight of the packages.

4. How do I track my deliveries?

You will be provided with an app that allows you to track your assigned deliveries, view customer details, and manage your routes efficiently.

5. What should I do if I am unable to complete a delivery?

In case of emergencies or if you are unable to complete a delivery, contact our support team immediately to inform them, and we will assist in rescheduling or reassigning the delivery.

6. How can I contact customer support if I have issues with a delivery?

You can contact our support team through the app or by calling our helpline. We are available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.

7. What happens if a customer is unavailable at the delivery location?

If the customer is unavailable, we will attempt to contact them. If we cannot reach them, the package will be returned, and you will be instructed on next steps.

8. Do I need to provide my own insurance?

No, we provide insurance coverage for your deliveries. However, we recommend that you keep your own vehicle insurance up to date.

9. How do I get paid for my deliveries?

Payments are made on a weekly basis through direct deposit or a payment app, depending on your preference.

10. Can I work part-time as a courier?

Yes, you can choose your working hours, making it easy to work part-time or on a flexible schedule.

11. Are there any additional benefits for couriers?

Couriers can earn bonuses for on-time deliveries, good customer feedback, and working during peak times.

12. What happens if I miss a delivery?

If you miss a delivery, contact support immediately. We will attempt to reschedule or assign the delivery to another courier if necessary.

13. Can I decline a delivery?

You can decline a delivery if it is outside your preferred working area or schedule. However, repeated declines may affect your status.

14. How do I report an accident or damage to the package?

If you encounter an accident or package damage, report it immediately to our support team, and we will handle the situation.

15. Can I deliver internationally as a courier?

Currently, we only offer domestic deliveries, but we plan to expand our courier services internationally in the future.
